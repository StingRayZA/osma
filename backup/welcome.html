Welcome to the Red Hat Open Source Maturity Assessment


The Red Hat Open Source Enablement Pathway provides Strategic management consulting around open source and communities.

To help formalize enterprise open source adoption, collaboration, and governance strategies, Red Hat offers the Pathway to Open Source Enablement. This Pathway provides enterprise organizations with proven practices and recommendations demonstrated by Red Hat to transform culture and processes to harness open source business value.

This strategic business consulting service:

  • Gives customers the tools and processes to safely, formally, and effectively consume open source technologies in their enterprise.
  • Mentors customers in effectively collaborating with or contributing to open source communities for their strategic benefit
  • Guides customers in creating strategic open source projects and communities to share development costs and risks.
